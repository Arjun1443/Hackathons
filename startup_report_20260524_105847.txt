**Best Structure: Private Limited Company**

**Why this is recommended for your Drone Startup:**

1.  **Fundraising:** Drones are capital-intensive (R&D, fleet purchase). A Private Limited Company is the only structure that allows you to issue Equity Shares to Angel Investors, VCs, or even apply for government grants (like the Drone Shakti scheme).

1.  **Obtain Digital Signature Certificate (DSC):** All proposed Directors need a Class 3 DSC. This is your digital signature for filing forms.

**Phase 2: Company Incorporation (MCA Portal)**

3.  **Name Reservation (RUN/SPICe+):** Propose 2-3 names. Ensure the name reflects your business (e.g., "SkyCart Logistics Private Limited"). Avoid words like "National," "India," or "Drone" without proper justification.
4.  **File SPICe+ Form (INC-32):** This is the master form. It includes:
    - MOA (Memorandum of Association) & AOA (Articles of Association).
    - Declaration of registered office address in Vijayawada.
    - Subscription to shares by the first directors.
5.  **Apply for PAN & TAN:** The SPICe+ form automatically applies for the company's Permanent Account Number (PAN) and Tax Deduction Account Number (TAN).

**Phase 3: Post-Incorporation (Operational Setup)**

6.  **Open a Current Bank Account:** Use the Certificate of Incorporation, PAN, and MOA/AOA to open a bank account in Vijayawada.
7.  **Register for GST:** Required immediately if your turnover is expected to cross ₹20 lakhs (or ₹40 lakhs for goods). Since you are providing a *service* (delivery), the threshold is ₹20 lakhs.
