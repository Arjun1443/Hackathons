# Market Research Report: Autonomous Drone Delivery for Medicines & Small Goods (Pilot Project)

## 1. CURRENT MARKET PROBLEMS (3-5 Specific Pain Points)

The "last-mile" delivery market is broken. Here are the specific pain points your drone solution must address:

**1. The "Cold Chain" Failure (Pharma Specific)**
- **Pain Point:** 20% of temperature-sensitive pharmaceuticals (vaccines, insulin, biologics) are degraded or destroyed during ground transport due to temperature excursions (heat/cold) and delays.
- **Specificity:** Traditional couriers (UPS, FedEx) use passive ice packs that fail in extreme weather. Refrigerated trucks are expensive ($150-$300 per stop) and inefficient for single-dose deliveries.
- **Impact:** $35 billion in annual pharmaceutical waste globally.

**2. The "Traffic Tax" on Urgent Deliveries**
- **Pain Point:** In urban areas, 30-40% of delivery time is spent idling in traffic. For emergency medicines (e.g., EpiPens, blood transfusions, anti-venom), a 30-minute delay can be fatal.
- **Specificity:** Ground couriers cannot bypass traffic. Motorcycles are faster but limited by weather and payload size.
- **Impact:** Hospitals lose an estimated $1.2 million annually per facility due to delayed lab results and medication shortages.

**3. The "Rural & Suburban Access Gap"**
- **Pain Point:** 60 million Americans live in "pharmacy deserts" (areas >10 miles from a pharmacy). Delivery costs to these areas are 3-5x higher than urban zones.
- **Specificity:** Traditional logistics require a driver to travel 30+ miles round trip for a single $10 prescription. This is economically unviable.
- **Impact:** 1 in 4 patients skip or delay medication due to access issues.

**4. The "Payload Fragility & Security" Problem**
- **Pain Point:** Small goods (electronics, glasses, documents) are often damaged or stolen during ground transit. "Porch piracy" costs $12 billion annually in the US.
- **Specificity:** Drones offer a "hand-off" to a secure locker or direct drop, eliminating the theft window. Ground couriers cannot guarantee this.
- **Impact:** 26% of consumers have had a package stolen in the last year.

**5. The "Regulatory & Noise" Barrier**
- **Pain Point:** Current drone delivery (e.g., Zipline, Wing) is loud (65-80 dB) and restricted to "visual line of sight" (VLOS) in most countries.
- **Specificity:** Your pilot project must solve for **Beyond Visual Line of Sight (BVLOS)** approval, which is the #1 bottleneck for scaling.
- **Impact:** 90% of drone delivery startups fail because they cannot get FAA/EASA waivers for BVLOS flight.
